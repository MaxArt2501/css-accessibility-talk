WCAG 2.1 requirements

Level AA

  • Large text: 3.0 ratio
  • Other text: 4.5 ratio
  • Graphical objs: 3.0 ratio

Level AAA

  • Large text: 4.5 ratio
  • Other text: 7.0 ratio
  • Graphical objs: no reqs
